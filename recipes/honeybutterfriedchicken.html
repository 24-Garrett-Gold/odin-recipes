Honey Butter Fried Chicken

Crispy Crunchy Honey Butter Fried Chicken

This honey butter fried chicken is so crispy and crunchy that it will for sure be a hit with family or friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup corn starch
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup ice cold water
  • 1/2 stick butter
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cut chicken thighs into medium size cubes
  2. Add cut up chicken to a bowl and mix together the flour, corn starch, baking soda, and your ice cold water
  3. Fry chicken at 350 degrees until golden brown
  4. For the sauce, add the butter, honey, sugar, and soy sauce into a pan. On medium heat, mix everything together until wel incorporated, then turn off the heat.
  5. Add chicken to pan and mix together until fully covered with the sauce.
  6. Enjoy!
